Arrows / right click drag - Move map
PageUp / PageDown / Mouse Wheel - Zoom
Home - Reset zoom
Left click - Draw tile
[ / ] - Choose tile
Ctrl - Toggle foreground / background
P - Pickup tile
Del - Clear tile
Shift - Make selection
Esc - Clear selection
A - Select all
D - Duplicate selection
X - Cut (move) selection
B - Clear (bin) selection
M - Duplicate mirrored
K - Duplicate flipped
L - Duplicate mirrored & flipped
1-9,-,= - Insert pepper
G - Toggle grid
H - Toggle help
R - Toggle play mode
# - Circulate cached maps
